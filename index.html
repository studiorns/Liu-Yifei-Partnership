Celebrity Influence Framework in Chinese Market

Source of Inspiration

13% of Chinese consumers identify celebrities' travels or endorsements as a source of travel inspiration. While other sources are more prevalent, celebrity influence is still a notable factor.

Higher Engagement

2.8 celebrities average engagement vs 1.8 for general influencers. Chinese travelers show stronger engagement with celebrities, making them key drivers of travel decisions.

Strong Admiration & Loyalty

Celebrities command unique admiration and loyalty that significantly surpasses typical influencer followings. Fan bases are more robust and passionate, making celebrities powerful conduits for shaping travel aspirations.

Strategic Performance Analysis

Engagement Rate Superiority

Liu Yifei campaigns achieved 49% higher engagement rates (2.67% average vs 1.79% average), demonstrating superior audience connection and content resonance with target demographics.

Quality Over Cost Efficiency

While non-celebrity campaigns achieved lower cost per view, Liu Yifei campaigns delivered significantly higher engagement quality, resulting in improved performance metrics.

Peak Performance Achievement

Summer 2024 with Liu Yifei achieved the highest engagement rate (3.26%), demonstrating that celebrity endorsement drives exceptional audience interaction and campaign effectiveness.

Viral Social Media Impact & "Liu Yifei Storm"

#1 Weibo Trending

Content Recreation
